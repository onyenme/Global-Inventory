# GLOBAL INVENTORY DASHBOARD

# Tools: 

a.Microsoft Excel 

b. Power BI 

c. DAX 

d. Data Modeling


# Focus:

i. Revenue

ii. Profitability 

iii. Operations.

  
  # Overview
This project involved designing a three-layer business intelligence dashboard which analyzed revenue performance, cost efficiency and operational effectiveness across a global inventory dataset.
The objective was to build a centralized decision-support system for executive-level insights.
  
 
   # The Business Problem
The organization lacked structured visibility into:
	
  a. 	Revenue trends across countries and suppliers.
	
  b.	Cost distribution and inefficiencies.

  c.	Inventory turnover and warehouse operations.
 
  d.  Decision-making was also fragmented and reactive.
 
 
 # The Solution
I developed a three-page Power BI dashboard structured into:
	
  a.	Revenue Intelligence
	
  b.	Profit & Cost Optimization
	
  c.	Operations & Inventory Performance
Each layer focuses on specific KPIs aligned with strategic business objectives.
 
 1.  Revenue  Layer
KPIs Designed:
	
  i.	Total Revenue
	
  ii.	Revenue per Order

  iii. High-Value Revenue Contribution
	
  iv. Revenue Efficiency
 The Insights :
	
  a. Identified revenue concentration risk in high-value segments
	
  b. Highlighted top-performing suppliers
	
  c. Revealed monthly revenue volatility patterns
 Business Impact:
It enabled the pricing evaluation, supplier strategy optimization, and regional performance monitoring.


2. Profit & Cost Optimization Layer
KPIs Designed:
	a.	Total Profit
	
  b.	Total Cost
	
  c.	Profit Margin
	
  d.	Cost Ratio
The Insights :
	
  i.	Strong margin performance identified
	
  ii.	Cost-heavy suppliers highlighted for negotiation
	
  iii.	High-performing product categories isolated
Business Impact:
It improved the cost management visibility and product profitability analysis.


3. Operations & Inventory Layer
KPIs Designed:
	
  a.	Inventory Turnover
	
  b.	Total Stock Quantity
	
  c.	High Inventory Risk
	
  d.	Average Processing Time
 The Insights:
	i.	Warehouse capacity imbalance detected
	
  ii.	Stock movement trends analyzed
	
  iii. Operational accuracy variation identified
Business Impact:
It contributed to warehouse optimization and demand planning decisions.


# Analytical Techniques Used 
	1. A Time-Series Trend Analysis.
	2. The Benchmark of KPI.
	3. Analysis based on Revenue Concentration.
	4. The Margin & Cost Ratio Evaluation.
	5. Inventory Turnover Analysis.
	

# Strategic Value
This dashboard integrates financial and operational metrics into a unified reporting framework which transforms raw data into structured form and decisions can be carried out accurately.
